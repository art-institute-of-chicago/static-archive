Directions
The Art Institute of Chicago and Symphony Center are located just a block away from each other on Michigan Avenue in Chicago's South Loop. They are easily accessible by public transportation or by car, with many parking options located within walking distance.

Public Transportation
You can also reach the Art Institute and Symphony Center by Metra and CTA bus and train lines. Taxis are almost always available in front of these South Loop institutions.

How to Buy Tickets

Visit: Tickets can be purchased at the Art Institute during regular business hours.
Call: Tickets can be purchased by calling (312) 575-8000. Tickets can be mailed to you if ordered 10 days prior to the event or may be picked up at the museum.  
Connect: Tickets can be purchased online. Tickets can be mailed to you if ordered 10 days prior to the event or may be picked up at the museum. Fees apply to phone and online orders.

For Events at Symphony Center

Call: (312) 294-3000. Ticketing Services hours are from 10:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. Monday through Saturday, and from 11:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. on Sunday during the regular concert season. Subscriptions and single tickets can also be purchased online at www.cso.org.
