Books

Many people might not think of the Art Institute as a publishing house, but the Publications Department, a publisher of books since the late 19th century, offers a wide variety of titles focused on the museum’s rich collection, history, and exhibitions. The books featured on this page demonstrate the breadth and depth of the museum’s collection of modern art and shed light on the development and influence of modernism. Whether the audience is children and families or students and scholars, these books provide a myriad of ways to look at modern.
