CONSERVATION of the Windows

The condition of each panel was carefully documented with photography and annotated diagrams before work was initiated; small cleaning tests identified a safe and effective method to treat the work. With a clear understanding of the unique manufacture and durable composition of the windows, conservators began their treatment. They also re-puttied the caming where necessary and repaired several small breaks that had occurred in travel from France and during the original installation in Chicago.
