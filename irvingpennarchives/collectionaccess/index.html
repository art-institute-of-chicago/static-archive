Collection Access

For information about accessing the Irving Penn Paper Archives, and related library resources, please see the Ryerson and Burnham Libraries for hours, contact information, and the library catalog. Please also see the finding aid to the Irving Penn Archives.

For information about accessing the material in the Irving Penn Photographic Archive as well as Penn's fine art prints in the museum's permanent collection, please see the Photography Study Room for information about scheduling an appointment.

For general information about visiting the museum, please see Visitor Information.
