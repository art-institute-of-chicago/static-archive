The items listed below are intended to supplement the material found in "The Magic of America." Unless otherwise noted, all downloadable files are in PDF format:

 

Pregliasco, Janice, "The Life and Work of Marion Mahony Griffin." – A journal article which provides a careful overview and analysis of MMG's achievements in architecture. Reprinted from Museum Studies (The Art Institute of Chicago) 21, no. 2 (1995): 164-181, 191-192.

Marion Mahony Griffin Timeline – A listing of important dates and events in MMG's life and career.

The Four Ethers and The Threefold Commonwealth – A brief introduction to the principles which Marion Mahony Griffin believed were a part of the physical and social worlds.

Essays Attributed to Walter Burley Griffin in "The Magic of America" – A listing of writings in "The Magic of America" that Marion Mahony Griffin states explicitly were written by Walter Burley Griffin.

Mossmain Brochures – Promotional materials for a planned town development in south central Montana (See III.3.42ff):

  • Yellowstone Garden City Holding Corporation. Mossmain, Montana. Spokane, Washington: Quick Print, 1914.
  • Town Plat of Mossmain, Montana [and] Mossmain, Montana: A Model City Built on Modern Plans. N.p., [ca. 1914].

"Scrapbook" – A collection of newspaper clippings relating to Canberra from 1914-1924. (The original is held by the New-York Historical Society.)

Castlecrag Brochures – Promotional material for a residential development, designed and built by the Griffins, approximately four miles from Sydney:

  • Castlecrag Album. [Sydney?]: s.n., [1926?].
  • Castlecrag Homes. Sydney: Boylan & Co. Ltd, [1927?].

Castlecrag Maps – Originally issued as supplements to The Crag – Castlecrag 1924-1938 (Blackheath: Brandl & Schlesinger, 2007). Courtesy of Wanda Spathopoulos:

  • Middle Harbour with relevant landmarks c. 1930.
  • Castlecrag and its surrounds 1926.
  • Castlecrag Peninsula 1930.

Griffin, Walter B. The UP [United Provinces] Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition, Lucknow: Album. Lucknow: The Exhibition, [1936]. – A selection of architectural plans and drawings for the Exposition held between December 1936 and February 1937 in what is now Uttar Pradesh, India. (A microform of the original is held by the Frances Loeb Library, Harvard University Graduate School of Design. Illustrations from this publication and other closely related images are found principally in Section I but also occur throughout the rest of "The Magic of America.")

Full-text PDF of "The Magic of America" – This is a downloadable text-only version without illustrations. The text is taken from the Digital Text section of this web site. Using Adobe Reader's built-in search function, it is useful for scholars who wish to search for words or phrases appearing in the manuscript.
