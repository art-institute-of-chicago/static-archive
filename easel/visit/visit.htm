Audio Tours
Narrated by exhibition curator Gloria Groom and Bonnard scholar Nicholas Watkins, audio tours may be rented at the exhibition entrance (members, $4; public, $5). An enlarged-type transcript of the tour, free to visually impaired or hearing impaired visitors, is available upon request.

Admission
The full suggested museum admission is required for entrance. Purchase tickets at any admission counter. Advance ticketing and timed entrances do not apply to this exhibition.

Members
Members must show their membership card to view Beyond the Easel. A reasonable number of guests may accompany them, based on the level of membership.
