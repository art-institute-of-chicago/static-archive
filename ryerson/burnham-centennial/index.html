Burnham Library Centennial

September 25–December 3, 2012
Ryerson & Burnham Libraries

Celebrating 100 years of the museum's Burnham Library of Architecture, this exhibition highlights archival collections received over the past 25 years, including correspondence, photographs, project files, sketchbooks, and realia from the collections of numerous artists and architects.
