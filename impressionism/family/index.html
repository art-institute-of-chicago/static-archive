Family Activities

These online art activities for families will help young people and their parents or caregivers enjoy and discover the many works of art in the collection of the Art Institute of Chicago. The projects can be done at home. Many of the supplies are household objects, and the steps are easy to follow. Teachers may wish to adapt some of these activities for the classroom as well. Be sure to plan a visit to the museum to see the actual works upon which these activities are based. We hope to see you often!
