Macedonia: area of ancient Greece north of Mt. Olympus; famous as the kingdom of Philip II and his son, Alexander the Great
maenads: female followers of the god Dionysos
Mars: Roman god of war; known as Ares by the Greeks
Meleager: mythical hero who led the hunt for the Calydonian Boar and who died in his youth
Memnon: king of Ethiopia; son of Eos, goddess of dawn; killed by Achilles in the Trojan War
Mercury: Roman name for the Greek god Hermes, messenger of the gods
