Near East: countries near the eastern end of the Mediterranean Sea, including Arabia, Egypt, Iran, Iraq, Israel, Jordan, Palestine, Syria, and Turkey

Neptune: Roman god of the sea; known as Poseidon to the Greeks
New Kingdom: Egyptian chronological term for Dynasties 17–20; around 1576–1069 B.C.
Nike: winged goddess of victory
obol: Greek coin whose value was one sixth of a drachma
