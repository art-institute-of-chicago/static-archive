Museum3D: IMLS Capstone Celebration

ckaufman
3 years ago

Wrapping up a year long project with support from the IMLS Sparks! Ignition grant, this video highlights the Museum3D project showcasing a variety of museum education programs using 3-D technologies to engage audiences with the museum’s collection. As a culminating event, the capstone celebration brought professionals from a diverse range of museums, universities, libraries, and zoos, as well as community and youth organizations at the local and regional level.
