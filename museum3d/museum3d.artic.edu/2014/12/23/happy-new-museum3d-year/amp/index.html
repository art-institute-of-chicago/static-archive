Happy New Museum3D Year!

Liz Neely
3 years ago

Thanks to everyone who followed along with our year of Museum3D programs at the Art Institute of Chicago. We couldn’t have done it without the generous support for innovative programs from the IMLS, the Art Institute for allowing us to experiment with new programs and the many talented collaborators along the way. The team has released the evaluation report hoping to help you incorporate 3D into museum and informal education programs. Enjoy a bit of Museum3D reading over the break and jump start 2015 with some 3D in your programming.

Happy holidays!
