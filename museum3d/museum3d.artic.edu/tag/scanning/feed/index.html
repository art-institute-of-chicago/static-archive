Many visitors got to experience 3-D technologies in action with 532 visitors dropping by the Ryan Education Center 3-D events and 139 visitors scanning in the Alsdorf gallery.

We’ll post more reflections to come from various viewpoints.  As a strong emphasis of our research is on evaluation, following are the instruments used to determine outcomes of the event.

  • Instrument 2: Visitor Observation (Ryan Education Center)
  • Instrument 3: Visitor Interview (Alsdorf Gallery)
